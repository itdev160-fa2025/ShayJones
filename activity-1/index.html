Welcome to the Official Page of Shay Jones!

Former Women's Basketball Student Athlete

Shay Jones was born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Shay Jones went to Whitefishbay Dominican High School. Dominican High School would win three Conference Championships in the four years she attended. Shay Jones would also lead Dominican High School to their first State Tournament since the 90s. Dominican Lady Knights would go to State 26-0. With a hard fought game, Dominican would lose their first game of the entire basketball season. Shay Jones then became the 5th player to score 1,000 points in their basketball career.
Shay Jones would later go on to Concordia University, Saint Paul on an four-year basketball scholarship. Jones would be the 27th player to score 1,000 points. Jones would also become the fifth player in history to record 1,000 points, 400 rebounds, 300 assists. Jones would finish her career eight all time with 365 assists.


Jones would graduate from Concordia University with a bachelor degree in Sport Management with two minors in marketing and coaching. Shay Jones would then go to work for the Milwaukee Brewers for 3 years as a Ticket Sales Representive. She enjoyed being able to change and make people's lives better.
